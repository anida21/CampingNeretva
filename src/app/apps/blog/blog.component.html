WELCOME TO CAMPING NERETVA

At the heart of Mostar is Camping Neretva, your gateway to unforgettable adventures. The camp offers the perfect blend of comfort and direct contact with nature. Situated right along the banks of the Neretva, the camp provides spectacular views and access to a variety of water activities.

Whether you enjoy camping under the open sky, exploring the surroundings on a bicycle, or relaxing to the sounds of the river, Camping Neretva promises a unique experience that will awaken your adventurous spirit. Visit us and experience the fusion of nature, culture, and excitement in the heart of this irresistible part of Bosnia and Herzegovina.
